Appetizers

  1. Bruschetta Trio: A medley of classic tomato, creamy goat cheese, and balsamic glaze on toasted baguette slices.
  2. Crispy Calamari: Lightly battered and fried calamari rings served with marinara sauce and lemon wedges.
  3. Caprese Salad: Fresh buffalo mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil leaves drizzled with balsamic reduction and extra virgin olive oil.
  4. Stuffed Mushrooms: Button mushrooms stuffed with herbed cream cheese, topped with breadcrumbs, and baked to perfection.
  5. Cajun Shrimp Skewers: Succulent shrimp seasoned with Cajun spices, skewered and grilled, served with a zesty remoulade sauce.

Entrees

  1. Grilled Salmon: Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet grilled to perfection, served with lemon-dill sauce and roasted vegetables.
  2. Chicken Marsala: Tender chicken breast sautéed with mushrooms in a Marsala wine sauce, served over creamy mashed potatoes.
  3. Vegetable Stir-Fry: A colorful array of seasonal vegetables stir-fried in a savory ginger-soy sauce, served over steamed jasmine rice.
  4. Penne alla Vodka: Penne pasta tossed in a creamy vodka tomato sauce with pancetta, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Filet Mignon: Prime beef tenderloin grilled to your liking, served with garlic mashed potatoes and sautéed asparagus.

Pizzas

  1. Margherita Pizza: Fresh tomato sauce, buffalo mozzarella, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil on a thin, crispy crust.
  2. BBQ Chicken Pizza: Tangy BBQ sauce, grilled chicken, red onions, cilantro, and mozzarella cheese on a hand-stretched dough.
  3. Mediterranean Veggie Pizza: Roasted red peppers, Kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, feta cheese, and spinach on a garlic-infused crust.
  4. Meat Lovers Pizza: Pepperoni, Italian sausage, bacon, and ham with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.
  5. Truffle Mushroom Pizza: Creamy white sauce, wild mushrooms, caramelized onions, Gruyere cheesen in a crispy crust.

Desserts

  1. Tiramisu: Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder, served chilled.
  2. New York Cheesecake: Creamy cheesecake with a graham cracker crust, topped with raspberry coulis and whipped cream.
  3. Chocolate Lava Cake: Warm chocolate cake with a gooey molten center, served with vanilla bean ice cream.
  4. Fruit Sorbet Trio: Assorted flavors of refreshing fruit sorbet, served in a crisp tuile cup.
  5. Crème Brûlée: Rich vanilla custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust, served with fresh berries.
